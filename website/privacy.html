Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Introduction

Nexa stores user data in your Firebase project. This policy explains what we collect and how it's used.

Data We Collect

  • Name & email (Auth)
  • Notes, tasks, reminders, cashbook entries

How We Use Your Data

Sync across devices, enable reminders, and provide reports.

Permissions

Camera for photos, SMS only if you enable SMS auto-add, notifications for reminders.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their account which will remove data from the app; data is stored in your Firebase project.
